Factors

Weather Forecast

Weather forecasts aren’t always spot-on.

Inaccurate predictions lead to errors in our model’s estimates.

The model adapts by adjusting predictions based on previous years patterns.

But its accuracy hinges on reliable weather data.

Humidity

The AI model predicts Delhi’s daily power consumption.

It struggled to provide accurate results due to variations in humidity.

Humidity significantly impacted electricity usage, particularly affecting cooling system demand (e.g., air conditioners).

Unexpected humidity changes led to inaccurate predictions.

Sunrise and Sunset

Sunset and sunrise times impact electricity usage, especially for lighting needs.

Inconsistent or inaccurate data for sunset and sunrise times can lead to incorrect assumptions about daylight duration.

When the model relies on incorrect timing data, its predictions about power consumption become less reliable.
